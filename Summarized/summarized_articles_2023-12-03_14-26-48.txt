Title: Is AI the answer to sustainable farming?
Date: 2023-11-14T13:00:00Z
URL: https://www.theverge.com/2023/11/14/23950666/ai-sustainable-farming-machine-learning-agriculture
Summarized Content: The article discusses the application of advanced technology, such as AI and robotics, in sustainable farming. One example is Zordi, an ag startup that uses AI and robotics to cultivate strawberries in greenhouses, with the aim of growing fresh, local produce sustainably. The integration of AI and machine learning in agriculture has a short history, with applications ranging from predictive analytics for crop yields to precise application of resources at the individual plant level. The use of AI in farming is seen as a powerful tool to help meet the needs of a growing global population sustainably. However, there are also potential drawbacks and risks, including concerns about data ownership, labor issues, security threats, and the digital divide. Overall, AI and ML are seen as powerful enablers for farmers to adapt to the challenges of climate change and increased demand for food.
